Returns

At Super Store, we are dedicated to ensuring our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, our Returns Policy provides a straightforward process for returning items.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Time Frame: Items can be returned within 3 days of the order date.
  • Condition: Items must be returned in their original condition, unused, and in their original packaging, with all tags and labels attached.
  • Proof of Purchase: A receipt or proof of purchase is required for all returns.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are not eligible for return, including:

  • Perishable goods
  • Personal care items
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software or digital products

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Customer Support: Contact our customer support team at superstore@hello.com or call +1 386-688-3295 to request a return authorization.
  2. Return the item safely in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and parts. Include the return authorization form provided by our customer support team.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item, please contact our customer support team to discuss the exchange process. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund and provide instructions for returning the damaged or defective product.
